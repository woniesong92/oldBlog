About

Hi, I am Howon, a CS undergraduate at Cornell University. I am the author of Chrome extension Instant Music and one of the top contributors to Mozilla's open source project TogetherJS. I now work at Lab80 as an intern, but I was involved in strategic management consulting and worked with professional consultants at Nomura Research Institute and PricewaterhouseCoopers in my early years in college. My dream is seeing people I meet on the bus or subway use my products. Before I reach 30, I am determined to start a startup.
