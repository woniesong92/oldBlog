Hi, I am Howon, a CS undergraduate at Cornell University.

I am the creator of a popular Chrome extension Instant Music and an admin of Mozilla's open source project TogetherJS. I recently worked at Lab80 as an intern, making Hello Money, a tool for designing investment portfolios. In my early years of college, I was involved in strategic management consulting and worked with professional consultants at Nomura Research Institute and PricewaterhouseCoopers. My dream is seeing people who are using my product when I get on the bus or subway. Before I reach 30, I am determined to start my own startup.
